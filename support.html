How the app works

Each day the app shows 5 short, hand-picked articles on the Home tab. You can also browse the full library by category, save articles as bookmarks, and read everything fully offline.

Subscription & free trial

The app starts with a 3-day free trial. After the trial, a subscription is required to keep reading. Three plans are available: 1 Month, 6 Months, and 1 Year. You can pick any of them on the subscription screen.

Restoring a purchase

If you already subscribed and don’t see the unlocked content (for example, on a new device), open the subscription screen and tap Restore in the top right corner. It will re-link your purchase to your Apple Account.

Managing or canceling a subscription

All subscriptions are processed by Apple and can be managed in your Apple Account settings on iPhone: Settings → your name at the top → Subscriptions → Become Smarter Daily. From there you can change plan, cancel, or review billing history at any time.

Refunds

Apple handles all refund requests for App Store purchases. You can request a refund at reportaproblem.apple.com.

Privacy

See our Privacy Policy for details on how the app handles data. In short: no account is required, no analytics or ads, and bookmarks stay on your device.
